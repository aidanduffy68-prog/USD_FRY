# GH Systems On-Chain Strategy
**Cryptographic Receipts, Not Systems**

## Core Principle

**We keep everything proprietary off-chain. We put only a tiny "proof the output is real" on-chain.**

This turns intelligence into verifiable objects that can be used everywhere.

---

## The Approach

### Off-Chain (Proprietary)
- ✅ All AI models and systems
- ✅ Full intelligence processing
- ✅ Behavioral signatures
- ✅ Threat dossiers
- ✅ Predictive models
- ✅ All proprietary logic

### On-Chain (Public Proof)
- ✅ Cryptographic receipt of intelligence output
- ✅ Proof that output is real/authentic
- ✅ Minimal metadata (just enough to verify)
- ✅ Timestamp and hash of intelligence package

---

## What This Enables

### 1. Verifiable Intelligence Objects
- Intelligence becomes a verifiable, cryptographic object
- Anyone can verify the intelligence came from GH Systems
- Proof of authenticity without revealing proprietary methods

### 2. Beyond Settlements → Workflows
- Not just payment/settlement mechanism
- Intelligence becomes usable in workflows
- Other systems can trust and use the intelligence
- Enables integration with other platforms

### 3. Usable Everywhere
- Cryptographic proof makes intelligence portable
- Can be used in any system that accepts verifiable data
- Not locked into a single platform
- Enables cross-platform intelligence sharing

---

## Key Benefits

1. **Privacy** - Proprietary systems stay private
2. **Verifiability** - Output is cryptographically provable
3. **Portability** - Intelligence can be used anywhere
4. **Trust** - Proof of authenticity without revealing methods
5. **Workflow Integration** - Not just payments, but actual intelligence workflows

---

## The Receipt Structure

**On-chain cryptographic receipt contains:**
- Hash of intelligence package
- Timestamp
- GH Systems signature
- Minimal metadata (actor ID, threat level, etc.)
- Proof of authenticity

**Off-chain intelligence package contains:**
- Full behavioral signature
- Complete threat dossier
- Predictive forecasts
- All proprietary analysis
- Full context and evidence

## Use Cases

### 2. Workflow Integration
