# How Research Firms Upsell Contracts with White-Label ABC
**The Specific Mechanics of Contract Upselling**

## The Upsell Transformation

**Before ABC:**
- Research firm offers: "Forensic reports" or "On-chain analysis"
- Contract value: $500K - $2M
- Deliverable: Historical analysis, "Here's what happened"

**After ABC (White-Label):**
- Research firm offers: "Predictive intelligence platform" or "Counterintelligence operations"
- Contract value: $1.5M - $3.5M (3x increase)
- Deliverable: Predictive targeting packages, "Here's what happens next and how to stop it"

---

## Specific Upsell Mechanics

### 1. Product Positioning Upsell

**From:** "Forensic Intelligence Reports"  
**To:** "Predictive Intelligence Platform" or "Counterintelligence Operations Platform"

**How it works:**
- Research firm brands ABC as their own platform
- They position it as their proprietary intelligence system
- Government sees it as a platform upgrade, not just reports
- Platform contracts are worth 3x more than report contracts

**Example:**
- Before: "We provide forensic reports on crypto threats" → $500K contract
- After: "We provide a predictive intelligence platform with counterintelligence capabilities" → $1.5M contract

---

### 2. Deliverable Transformation

**Before ABC:**
- Deliverable: PDF reports, spreadsheets, raw data
- Value: Historical analysis, "what happened"
- Pricing: Per-report or per-hour basis

**After ABC:**
- Deliverable: Executable targeting packages, threat dossiers, predictive intelligence
- Value: Predictive intelligence, "what happens next"
- Pricing: Platform license + intelligence packages

**Specific deliverables that justify upsell:**
- **Threat dossiers** (like ALPHA_47 example) - comprehensive counterintelligence packages
- **Targeting packages** - "Freeze Exchange_A/B within 24h; confidence 0.84"
- **Predictive forecasts** - "Off-ramp attempt in 48-72 hours at Binance.ae"
- **Counterintelligence analysis** - Hidden relationships, coordination networks
- **Regional intelligence** - Middle East focus, regional exchange data

---

### 3. Value Proposition Shift

**Before ABC:**
- Value: "We analyze what happened"
- Government need: Historical forensics
- Contract type: Service contract (reports)

**After ABC:**
- Value: "We predict what happens next and how to stop it"
- Government need: Pre-emptive action, targeting packages
- Contract type: Platform + intelligence contract

**The shift:**
- From reactive (what happened) → proactive (what happens next)
- From analysis → actionable intelligence
- From reports → executable packages
- From forensics → counterintelligence operations
